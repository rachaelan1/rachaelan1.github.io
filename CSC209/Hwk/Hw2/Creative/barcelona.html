Barcelona

Barcelona, Spain's second-most populous city, is home to over 6.9 million residents. As a major cultural and economic hub of Europe, it has hosted the Summer Olympics and numerous international sports tournaments. The city is renowned for its architectural wonders, including Park Guell, Casa Batllo, and the Basilica de la Sagrada Familia --UNESCO World Heritage Sites --along with Casa Mila, the Arc de Triomf, and Barceloneta Beach.
