Madrid

Madrid, the capital of Spain, is home to over 10.4 million residents. Known for its rich history and vibrant culture, the city is also famous for its legendary football club, Real Madrid. Some of its most iconic attractions include Plaza Mayor, the Royal Palace of Madrid, Buen Retiro Park, the Prado Museum, and the Reina Sofia Museum.
